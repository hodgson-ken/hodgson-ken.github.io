Ice Again

As of early Wednesday, over a foot of snow has been reported in parts of Franklin, Greenville, and Springfield.

The top snowfall total so far is 29.5 inches in Franklin, MN. Additional snowfall reports can be found toward the bottom of this article in the recap section.

New one-day March snowfall records were set on Tuesday in Springfield where 14.5 inches fell and in Greenville where 21.8 inches accumulated.

Winds have gusted from 35 to 50 mph Tuesday at Greenville's Greenway Airport. Midday Tuesday, winds gusted as high as 65 mph in East Franklin, while Springfield, clocked a 79-mph wind gust and Greenville gusted to 77 mph. Reports of trees downed have come in from the entire Tri-Cities area.
